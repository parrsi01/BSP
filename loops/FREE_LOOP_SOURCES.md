# Free & Legal Loop Sources for House Music

All sources listed here are royalty-free or Creative Commons licensed. Always verify the license for individual files before commercial use.

---

## Platform Directory

### 1. Splice — [splice.com](https://splice.com)
*Requires account + subscription*

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| Cost | ~$7.99–$13.99/month (credit-based) |
| License | Royalty-free, cleared for commercial use |
| Best for | Professional-quality one-shots, loops, drum hits |
| House-specific | Filter by genre: "House", "Tech House", "Deep House", "Afro House" |

**Search terms for house:** `house kick`, `deep house bass`, `tech house loop`, `house chord stab`, `soulful house vocal`, `afro house perc`

**Pro tip:** Save samples to your collection before your subscription lapses — you keep everything you've credited.

| Detail | Info |

### 4. Looperman — [looperman.com](https://www.looperman.com)
*Free, community-contributed*

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| Cost | Free |
| License | Royalty-free (each loop has individual terms — check before use) |
| Best for | Finding unique community-made loops; vocal samples; guitar loops |
| House-specific | Large library, filter by BPM (120–132) and genre |

**Search terms:** `house`, `deep house`, `tech house`, `house bass`, `house chord`, `house vocal`, `house drums 128bpm`

**Workflow tip:** Filter by BPM to find loops that match your project tempo. Download multiple options and audition in-DAW before committing.

### 6. Freesound — [freesound.org](https://freesound.org)
*Free, requires account*

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| Cost | Free (account required to download) |
| License | Creative Commons — **check each file** (CC0, CC-BY, CC-BY-NC) |
| Best for | Unique sound design elements, ambient textures, FX |
| House-specific | Search for risers, impacts, crowd noise, organic percussion |

**License key:**
- **CC0** — use freely, no credit needed
- **CC-BY** — credit the creator
- **CC-BY-NC** — non-commercial only — **do NOT use in sold beats**

**Search terms:** `house music loop`, `kick drum 909`, `house bass`, `clap`, `hi hat loop`, `dance percussion`, `riser electronic`
