# SoundCloud Analytics — Reading Data & Making Decisions

---

## 1. Free Analytics (No Pro Needed)

Every SoundCloud account, even free accounts, has access to basic track-level data:

| Metric | Available on Free | Location |
|--------|------------------|----------|
| Total plays per track | Yes | Track stats button (graph icon) |
| Total likes per track | Yes | Track page |
| Total reposts per track | Yes | Track page |
| Total comments | Yes | Track page |
| Follower count | Yes | Profile page |
| Play count history (limited) | Partial — last 30 days only | Track stats |

**Limitation of free analytics:** No geographic data, no traffic source data, no comparison over time beyond 30 days. For serious growth work, SoundCloud Pro is necessary.

## 2. Pro Analytics Dashboard — Every Metric Explained

Access at: soundcloud.com/[yourname]/stats

### Overview Metrics

| Metric | What It Means |
|--------|--------------|
| Total plays | All plays across all tracks in the selected period |
| Unique listeners | Individual users who played at least one track |
| Total likes | All likes received across all tracks |
| Total reposts | All reposts received across all tracks |
| Followers gained | Net follower change in the period |
| Download count | Number of tracks downloaded (if enabled) |

### Track-Level Metrics (click any track in the stats view)

| Metric | What It Means |
|--------|--------------|
| Plays timeline | Graph of plays over time — spot spikes and decay |
| Play source breakdown | Where plays came from (see Traffic Sources section) |
| Geographic breakdown | Top countries for plays |
| Plays vs listeners | How many times the average listener played this track |

## 3. Key Metrics and What They Mean

### Plays vs Unique Listeners

**Formula:** Plays ÷ Unique Listeners = Replay Ratio

| Replay Ratio | Interpretation |
|-------------|----------------|
| 1.0–1.2 | Each listener plays once — low retention |
| 1.3–1.7 | Healthy — some listeners replay |
| 1.8–2.5 | Strong — listeners are coming back to this track |
| 2.5+ | Exceptional — this track has fans, not just listeners |

**Decision:** If replay ratio is high on a specific track, this beat type resonates deeply. Make more in this style. Feature this track in artist DMs ("my fans replay this one").

---

### Likes-to-Plays Ratio (Engagement Rate)

**Formula:** Likes ÷ Plays × 100 = Like Rate %

| Like Rate | Benchmark | Interpretation |
|-----------|-----------|----------------|
| Under 1% | Below average | Track isn't resonating; check title/tags/quality |
| 1–2% | Average | Normal performance for beats |
| 2–5% | Good | Track is genuinely connecting with listeners |
| 5–10% | Excellent | Strong track — prioritize for promotion |
| 10%+ | Viral potential | Push this track aggressively |

**Context for beats:** Beats have structurally lower like rates than songs because many listeners are in browsing mode (searching catalog, not committing). A 2% like rate on a beat is strong.

---

### Reposts-to-Plays Ratio

**Formula:** Reposts ÷ Plays × 100 = Repost Rate %

| Repost Rate | Interpretation | Action |
|-------------|----------------|--------|
| Under 0.5% | Below average | Improve track or outreach strategy |
| 0.5–1% | Average | Normal organic repost rate |
| 1–2% | Good — listeners are sharing | Pitch to curators aggressively |
| 2%+ | Viral signal — listeners believe in this track | Promote immediately, pitch editorial |

**The repost rate is your virality indicator.** A high repost rate means listeners are actively promoting your track to their networks without you asking.

---

### Comments-to-Plays Ratio

**Formula:** Comments ÷ Plays × 100 = Comment Rate %

| Comment Rate | Interpretation |
|-------------|----------------|
| Under 0.1% | No community — listeners don't feel connected |
| 0.1–0.5% | Normal for instrumentals/beats |
| 0.5–1% | Good engagement — listeners are invested |
| 1%+ | Strong community — this track generates conversation |

Beats have inherently lower comment rates than songs. Focus on engineering the comment rate up through your pinned comment question (see SC_HACK_VAULT.md #6).

---

### Download Rate

**Formula:** Downloads ÷ Plays × 100 = Download Rate %

| Download Rate | Interpretation |
|--------------|----------------|
| 0% | No download enabled or no one wants it |
| 1–5% | Normal for free beats with gate |
| 5–10% | Strong demand — listeners want to keep this |
| 10%+ | Excellent — this beat type should be featured more |

High download rate + high plays = a winning free beat concept. Use the same style for your next free beat.

---

### Followers Gained Per Track

Track this manually: note how many followers you gained in the 48 hours after each upload.

**Benchmark:** If an upload consistently generates 10+ new followers, it's your "magnet" content — the style that brings people to your profile and converts them to followers.

**Decision:** Identify your top 3 "follower magnet" tracks. Make more in that style. Promote these specifically to cold audiences (external links, curator pitches) because they convert listeners to followers at a higher rate.

## 4. Geographic Data

SC Pro shows plays by country. This data is actionable:

| If your plays are concentrated in... | Consider |
|--------------------------------------|---------|
| UK | Target UK-based artists in DMs; run SC ads targeting UK |
| USA | Standard market — no change needed |
| Jamaica / Caribbean | Lean harder into trap dancehall content |
| Nigeria / Ghana / South Africa | Afro house, afrobeats crossover content |
| Germany / Netherlands | Deep house and tech house content performs here |
| France | Afro house and organic house audiences are strong |

**Promotion applications:**
- If 40% of your plays come from Nigeria and you're promoting on Instagram — target Nigeria in your Instagram ad settings
- If UK plays are rising, pitch UK-based curators specifically
- If a country appears with significant plays but you've done no specific outreach there, it's organic discovery — lean into it

## 5. Traffic Sources — What Each Source Means and How to Grow It

SoundCloud Pro shows where every play came from:

| Source | What It Means | How to Grow It |
|--------|--------------|----------------|
| sc/stream | SoundCloud feed (followers' stream) | More uploads, repost network |
| sc/search | Found via SC search | Better SEO: title, tags, description |
| sc/profile | Visited your profile directly | Better profile conversion + external social |
| sc/repost | Came from a repost | Expand repost network |
| sc/playlist | Found in a playlist | More playlist placements |
| sc/suggested | Algorithm's Suggested Tracks | Higher engagement rate, genre accuracy |
| external | Came from outside SC | More social links, Reddit, embeds |
| sc/embed | Played via embedded SC player | More external embeds |
| direct | Typed URL or bookmarked | High-intent repeat visitors — nurture these |

**Priority diagnosis:**
- If "sc/search" is under 10% of plays: your SEO is poor — fix titles and tags immediately
- If "external" is 0%: you are not promoting off-platform — start today
- If "sc/repost" is 0%: you have no repost network — build one this week
- If "sc/suggested" is growing: your engagement rate is high — the algorithm is working for you

## 6. Weekly Analytics Review Process

Run this every Sunday. Time required: 10–15 minutes.

**Step 1: Pull top-level numbers**
- Total plays this week vs last week (+ or -)
- New followers this week
- Total likes + reposts received

**Step 2: Identify your best track this week**
- Which track got the most plays?
- What was its engagement rate (likes/plays)?
- What traffic source drove most of its plays?

**Step 3: Identify your worst track**
- Which recent track underperformed?
- Check its traffic source split — is search low? Fix the SEO.
- Check its engagement rate — if under 1%, the track may need a retitle or should be moved to a back catalog playlist

**Step 4: One decision**
Make exactly one decision per week based on the data. Examples:
- "My 'tech house' tracks consistently outperform 'afro house' — shift production focus 60% tech house"
- "External traffic is 0% — I will post on Reddit every Sunday from now on"
- "Repost traffic is 5% — add 3 more members to my repost network this week"

## 7. Identifying Your Best-Performing Beat Type

After 20+ uploads, your data tells a clear story. Pull a comparison:

| Track | Genre | BPM | Plays | Like Rate | Repost Rate | Traffic Source Winner |
|-------|-------|-----|-------|-----------|-------------|----------------------|
| Track 1 | Afro House | 122 | 2,400 | 3.5% | 1.2% | SC Search |
| Track 2 | Tech House | 128 | 890 | 1.1% | 0.4% | Feed |
| Track 3 | Afro House | 124 | 1,800 | 2.8% | 0.9% | SC Search |
| Track 4 | Trap Dancehall | 140 | 3,100 | 4.1% | 2.1% | External |

**Pattern reading:** In this example, trap dancehall has the highest plays and repost rate, and external traffic is driving it — meaning social posts are working for dancehall specifically. Decision: increase trap dancehall production and social posting for dancehall tracks.

## 8. A/B Testing Beats with Analytics

You cannot run native A/B tests on SC, but you can simulate them:

**Upload-pair testing:**
1. Upload two variations of a beat concept on different days (same week)
2. Give both identical promotion (same repost network, same pitch to 5 curators each)
3. After 14 days, compare: plays, like rate, repost rate, follower gain
4. The winner's characteristics (BPM, mood, genre, title format) inform future production

**What to test:**
- 122 BPM afro house vs 126 BPM afro house (tempo preference)
- Vocal sample vs no vocal sample
- Punchy drums vs more melodic focus
- Longer intro (16 bars) vs shorter intro (8 bars)

## 9. When to Delete a Track

Not every track should live on your profile forever. A catalog full of underperforming tracks drags your overall engagement rate, which affects your account-level algorithm ranking.

**Delete threshold — a track meets ALL of these:**
- Under 100 plays after 6+ months live
- 0 likes, 0 reposts, 0 comments
- Audio quality is embarrassing compared to your current standard
- You've tried metadata refresh and it didn't move the needle

**Do not delete if:**
- Track has any plays at all and doesn't hurt your brand image
- Track is in someone else's playlist (deleting breaks their playlist)
- Track has a comment — someone cared enough to engage

**Alternative to deletion:** Set to Private (not deleted). SC retains play count history, and you can make it public again later.

## 11. Third-Party Analytics Stack

Supplement SC's native analytics with these free tools:

| Tool | What It Adds | Cost |
|------|-------------|------|
| Chartmetric (free tier) | Cross-platform play tracking, SC follower history, playlist tracking | Free (limited) |
| Soundsgood.co | Playlist analytics — see which playlists your tracks are in, follower counts | Free tier |
| Google Analytics | Traffic analytics for your Carrd/beat store — see which SC tracks drive store visits | Free |
| Spotify for Artists | If distributed — compare SC vs Spotify audience profiles | Free |
| Social Blade | SC account history over time (independent tracking) | Free |

**Chartmetric setup for SC:**
1. Create free account at Chartmetric
2. Claim your SC artist profile
3. Chartmetric tracks your SC play history, playlist placements, and cross-platform performance over time — useful for the longer view that SC's 30-day analytics window misses

**Google Analytics + Carrd integration:**
- Add GA4 tracking code to your Carrd beat store
- Tag every SC description's BeatStars link with UTM parameters by track
- In GA4, see which SC tracks drive the most beat store visits — this is your true conversion funnel data

## Analytics Decision Framework

| Data Signal | Decision |
|-------------|---------|
| Like rate drops below 1% on 3 consecutive tracks | Change beat style or improve audio quality |
| Search traffic under 10% of total plays | Fix all titles and tags this week |
| Repost rate 2%+ on any track | Promote this track with SC ads immediately |
| Geographic spike in a new country | Create content targeting that market |
| Engagement rate drops account-wide | Check for and stop any behavior triggering shadowban |
| Follower growth flat for 4+ weeks | Add 5 new repost network members; increase comment activity |
| External traffic = 0% | Start posting on Reddit, TikTok, and Facebook groups today |
