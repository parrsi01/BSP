# Beat Sales on SoundCloud

## Converting Plays Into Sales — The Core Funnel

```
Play → Hook in first 4 bars → Description CTA → Bio link → BeatStars/Airbit purchase
```

Every element below serves this funnel. A track with no CTA is a missed sale.

---

## Track Description Template (copy for every upload)

```
🔥 [Beat Name] | [BPM] BPM | [Key] | [Subgenre] Type Beat

Available for licensing:
💰 Lease: $30 | Unlimited: $100 | Exclusive: $300+
🎧 Purchase + stems → [your BeatStars link]
📩 Exclusive inquiries: [your email]

Free download for demo use only. Commercial use requires a license.

Tags: #housemusic #deephouse #typebeat #[artistname]typebeat #housebeat #producerlife
```

**Rules:**
- BeatStars/Airbit link goes in description AND as the track's "Buy" link
- Set "Buy" link in track settings → points directly to the beat's purchase page
- Free download ON for non-exclusive — drives listens + email if using a gate

---

## SoundCloud "Buy" Link Setup

1. Upload track → Edit track
2. Scroll to "Metadata" → "Buy link"
3. Paste the direct purchase URL (BeatStars individual beat link, not homepage)
4. Label it: "Buy License / Stems"
5. This shows as a button directly under the waveform — highest-visibility CTA on SC

## Free Download Gate Strategy (Drives Email + Plays)

Use a download gate service to require action before download:

| Tool | Gate Action | Cost |
|------|------------|------|
| ToneDen (Followgates) | Follow + repost required | Free |
| Feature.fm | Follow, Spotify save, email | Free tier |
| Hypeddit | Follow + repost | Free |
| Show.co | Email signup | Free tier |

**Flow:**
1. Mark track as "Free download" on SoundCloud
2. Download URL → your gate link (not direct WAV)
3. User must follow your SC + repost track to get the free WAV
4. Every free download = 1 new follower + 1 repost + potential future buyer

## Pricing on SoundCloud (What Works)

Display pricing in description. Buyers browse SC then purchase externally.

| License Tier | Recommended Price | What's Included |
|-------------|------------------|----------------|
| Basic Lease | $25–$35 | MP3, 50K streams, no stems |
| Premium Lease | $45–$65 | WAV + MP3, 100K streams, no stems |
| Unlimited Lease | $100–$150 | WAV + stems, unlimited streams |
| Exclusive | $300–$750 | Full rights, removed from store |

**Anchor pricing trick:** Show all 3 tiers in description. Mid-tier gets most sales.

## Beat Tags That Drive Search Traffic

SoundCloud's search is tag-powered. Use all 5 tag slots:

### Genre Tags (pick your subgenre)
`deep house` `tech house` `afro house` `soulful house` `house music`

### Artist Influence Tags (what artists would rap/sing over this)
`fisher type beat` `chris lake type beat` `black coffee type beat` `peggy gou type beat`

### Mood/Vibe Tags
`late night` `melodic` `dark` `groovy` `minimal`

**Do not waste tags on:** `beats for sale`, `buy beats` — no one searches these on SC.

## Playlist Strategy for Sales

Create themed playlists that group beats by mood/subgenre:

| Playlist Name | Purpose |
|--------------|---------|
| "Deep House Beats 2025 — For Sale" | SEO + catalog showcase |
| "Tech House Type Beats" | Targets tech house artists |
| "Free House Beats [Month Year]" | Drives plays + follows |
| "BSP Premium — Exclusive Beats" | Signals value, price anchors |

- Add new beats to relevant playlist on upload day
- Playlists rank in SoundCloud search — more entry points to your catalog

## Direct Artist Outreach on SoundCloud

1. Search your genre → filter "Tracks" → sort by "Recent"
2. Find artists (not producers) uploading original music in your style
3. Check their follower count: 500–10K is the sweet spot (hungry, not signed)
4. Leave a genuine comment on their track
5. DM: "Yo, I produce [genre] — made something that fits your style. Mind if I send a link?"
6. Follow up once if no reply after 5 days. Move on after that.

**DM template:**
```
Hey [name], love the vibe on [track name].
I produce deep house/[genre] and just finished something that would fit your sound.
Open to checking it out? No pressure — link: [SC track link]
```

## Beat Tape / Compilation Strategy

A beat tape (10–15 beats in one upload or playlist) performs differently than singles:

- Upload as individual tracks first (each gets own SEO)
- Create playlist as "BSP Vol.1 — House Beat Tape"
- Also upload a single mixed-down beat tape WAV for the "full listen" experience
- Beat tapes signal professionalism to artists shopping for exclusives
- Price beat tape bundles: "All 10 beats — $199 exclusive bundle"
