# Essential Plugins and VSTs for House Music Production

---

## Free Synthesizers

### Vital — [vital.audio](https://vital.audio)
| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| Type | Wavetable synthesizer |
| Format | VST3, AU, AAX, standalone |
| OS | Windows, Mac, Linux |
| Free tier | Yes (basic version fully functional) |

The most powerful free synth available. Comparable to Serum in capability. Excellent for house pads, bass, leads, and sound design. The wavetable morphing creates evolving pads perfect for deep house.

**House use cases:** Pads, leads, bass, arps, stabs

---

### OB-Xd — [discodsp.com/ob-xd](https://www.discodsp.com/ob-xd/)
| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| Type | Oberheim OB-X emulation |
| Format | VST, AU, AAX |
| Cost | Free |

Vintage Oberheim-style polyphonic synth. The lush, warm pads that come from OB-Xd are iconic in classic house music. Load it up, play a minor 7th chord, add reverb — instant deep house atmosphere.

**House use cases:** Lush pads, string-style chords, classic stabs

---

### Dexed — [asb2m10.github.io/dexed](https://asb2m10.github.io/dexed/)
| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| Type | Yamaha DX7 FM synth emulation |
| Format | VST, AU, standalone |
| Cost | Free |

Faithful DX7 emulation. The DX7 was everywhere in early house music (1985–1992). Electric pianos, metallic stabs, bell sounds. Vintage Chicago house and garage house sounds live here.

**House use cases:** Electric piano stabs, bell arpeggios, vintage house sounds, classic house bass

---

### Surge XT — [surge-synthesizer.github.io](https://surge-synthesizer.github.io)
| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| Type | Hybrid synthesizer (subtractive + wavetable + FM + formant) |
| Format | VST3, AU, CLAP, standalone |
| Cost | Free, open-source |

Extremely powerful and deep. Has a large preset library covering almost every style. The modulation routing is flexible enough for advanced sound design.

**House use cases:** Any synthesis type — especially good for bass and experimental sound design

---

### TAL-NoiseMaker — [tal-software.com](https://tal-software.com/products/tal-noisemaker)
| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| Type | Virtual analog synthesizer |
| Format | VST, AU |
| Cost | Free |

Simple, CPU-efficient virtual analog. Great for quick basses, basic leads, and simple pads. Its simplicity makes it a "just works" synth for house music fundamentals.

## Free Effects Plugins

### Valhalla SuperMVB — [valhalladsp.com](https://valhalladsp.com/shop/reverb/valhalla-supermvb/)
| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| Type | Reverb |
| Cost | Free |

Valhalla's free reverb plugin. High-quality algorithmic reverb covering rooms, halls, and plates. For house music pads and vocals, this sounds as good as expensive paid alternatives.

---

### TDR Nova — [tokyodawn.net](https://www.tokyodawn.net/tdr-nova/)
| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| Type | Parallel dynamic EQ |
| Cost | Free (GE version paid) |

One of the best free EQs available. Supports dynamic EQ operation (bands respond to the signal level). Excellent for gentle mixing EQ and mastering work.

---

### Kilohearts Snap Heap — [kilohearts.com](https://kilohearts.com/products/snap_heap)
| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| Type | Modular effects chain |
| Cost | Free (with individual snapin modules) |

A modular effects host. Many individual Kilohearts "snapins" (effects modules) are available free, including chorus, flanger, reverb, and distortion. Build custom FX chains.

---

### MFreeFXBundle — [meldaproduction.com](https://www.meldaproduction.com/MFreeFXBundle)
| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| Type | Suite of 40+ free plugins |
| Cost | Free |

Melda Production's entire free bundle. Includes: compressor, gate, EQ, reverb, delay, chorus, flanger, saturation, stereo expander, and many more. The MAnalyzer (spectrum analyzer) and MLoudnessAnalyzer (LUFS meter) are particularly useful.

## Paid Synthesizers (Worth It)

### Serum (Xfer Records) — [xferrecords.com/serum](https://xferrecords.com/products/serum)
| Detail | Price |
|--------|-------|
| Type | Wavetable synthesizer |
| Cost | $189 one-time |

The industry standard wavetable synthesizer. Virtually every professional house, techno, and electronic producer uses it. Massive preset marketplace. The visual display makes sound design intuitive.

**Why it's worth it:** Unprecedented control, the largest preset community, and it defines the sound of modern electronic music.

---

### Kontakt (Native Instruments) — [native-instruments.com](https://www.native-instruments.com/en/products/komplete/samplers/kontakt-7/)
| Detail | Price |
|--------|-------|
| Type | Sample library player and synthesizer |
| Cost | $199 ($49 on sale; NI regularly discounts) |

Required for most third-party sample libraries. Allows detailed sample manipulation, scripted instruments, and huge sound libraries. Much of the premium drum, piano, and orchestral content requires Kontakt.

---

### FabFilter Pro-Q3 — [fabfilter.com](https://www.fabfilter.com/products/pro-q-3-equalizer-plug-in)
| Detail | Price |
|--------|-------|
| Type | Parametric EQ |
| Cost | $179 |

The best EQ on the market. Transparent, surgical, and musical. Dynamic EQ and linear phase modes. The visual spectrum display makes EQ decisions intuitive. Industry standard for mixing and mastering.

---

### FabFilter Pro-C2 — [fabfilter.com](https://www.fabfilter.com/products/pro-c-2-compressor-plug-in)
| Detail | Price |
|--------|-------|
| Type | Compressor |
| Cost | $179 |

Eight compression styles in one plugin (Clean, Classic, Opto, Vocal, Mastering, Bus, Punch, Pumping — the "Pumping" style is perfect for house sidechain). Excellent metering and transparency.

---

### Valhalla Room — [valhalladsp.com](https://valhalladsp.com/shop/reverb/valhalla-room/)
| Detail | Price |
|--------|-------|
| Type | Algorithmic reverb |
| Cost | $50 |

Valhalla's flagship paid reverb. 12 room algorithms from small rooms to huge cathedrals. Used on virtually every professional recording. Also consider **Valhalla Plate** ($50) for drum and vocal reverb specifically.

---

### Arturia V Collection — [arturia.com](https://www.arturia.com/products/software-instruments/v-collection)
| Detail | Price |
|--------|-------|
| Type | Bundle of 30+ vintage synthesizer emulations |
| Cost | $499 (often on sale for $199–$299) |

Includes emulations of: Minimoog, Juno-106, Jupiter-8, DX7, OB-Xa, Prophet-5, ARP 2600, and more. For classic house sounds, the **Juno-106 V** and **Jupiter-8 V** are essential. Regularly discounted.

## Drum Machines and Sample Libraries

### Steven Slate Drums Free — [stevenslatedrums.com](https://www.stevenslatedrums.com/ssd5/#SSD5FREE)
| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| Type | Drum sample library |
| Cost | Free (limited kit) |
| Format | VST, AU (requires Kontakt-style player) |

Professional multi-sampled drums. The free version gives you one kit. Sounds significantly more realistic than basic samples. For house music, use sparingly — house often prefers electronic drum sounds.

---

### Roland Cloud (808/909 Emulation) — [roland.com/us/categories/software/roland-cloud/](https://www.roland.com/us/categories/software/roland-cloud/)
| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| Type | Virtual drum machines (TR-808, TR-909 emulations) |
| Cost | Subscription from $9.99/month or $200–$300 one-time |

The TR-808 and TR-909 are the defining drum machines of house music. Roland's official software emulations (TR-808, TR-909, TR-606) are some of the best emulations available. A free trial is available.

---

### Addictive Drums 2 (XLN Audio) — [xlnaudio.com](https://www.xlnaudio.com/products/addictive_drums_2)
| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| Type | Drum sample library with MIDI patterns |
| Cost | Free core + paid expansion packs |

Good for live-sounding drums. Less relevant for classic electronic house (909 sounds) but useful for organic, soulful house tracks with real-sounding percussion elements.

## Plugin Acquisition Tips

### Getting Plugins on a Budget
1. **BeatStars Plugin Boutique:** Free plugin of the month — check monthly
2. **AudioPluginDeals.com:** Sales up to 90% off regularly
3. **Plugin Boutique:** Loyalty points on purchases; frequent bundles
4. **iZotope sales:** Ozone (mastering suite) goes on sale 4–5x per year for $50–$100
5. **NI Summer/Winter sale:** Komplete bundles deeply discounted twice a year
6. **Group buys:** KVR Audio Forum hosts occasional group buys for discounted plugins

### Plugin Priority for a New Producer

| Priority | Plugin | Why |
|---------|--------|-----|
| 1 | Vital (free) | Complete sound design capability immediately |
| 2 | Valhalla SuperMVB (free) | Reverb solved for free |
| 3 | OB-Xd (free) | Authentic vintage house pads |
| 4 | TDR Nova (free) | Professional-grade EQ |
| 5 | Serum (paid) | When you can afford it — upgrade from Vital |
| 6 | FabFilter Pro-Q3 (paid) | When mixing quality becomes the limiting factor |
| 7 | Valhalla Room (paid) | When you need more reverb variety |
