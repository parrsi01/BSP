# Cover Art Creation Guide

---

## Specifications

| Use Case | Dimensions | Format | Min Resolution |
|----------|-----------|--------|---------------|
| Streaming (Spotify, Apple) | 3000 x 3000 px | JPEG | 72 dpi (screen) |
| BeatStars / Airbit | 1400 x 1400 px | JPEG | 72 dpi |
| YouTube thumbnail | 1280 x 720 px | JPEG | 72 dpi |
| Instagram feed post | 1080 x 1080 px | JPEG or PNG | 72 dpi |
| Physical print (if needed) | 3000 x 3000 px | PNG or TIFF | 300 dpi |

**Rule:** Always create at 3000x3000px and scale down — never scale up.

## Color Theory for House Music Aesthetics

### Dark / Underground / Tech House
- **Primary palette:** Near-black backgrounds (#0A0A0A), deep navy (#050F1A), charcoal (#1C1C1E)
- **Accent colors:** Electric blue (#00A6FB), cyan (#00FFFF), toxic green (#00FF41)
- **Feel:** Club basement, late night, industrial

### Deep House / Soulful
- **Primary palette:** Warm earth tones, amber (#D4A843), terracotta (#C06C4A), muted gold
- **Accent:** Soft orange, burgundy, forest green
- **Feel:** Warm, intimate, jazz-influenced

### Afro House
- **Primary palette:** Rich earth, deep orange (#E05C00), ochre, turquoise
- **Accent:** Bright yellows, bold reds
- **Feel:** Cultural richness, energy, sunsets

### Minimal / Modern House
- **Primary palette:** White, off-white (#F5F5F5), light gray
- **Accent:** Single bold color (red, electric blue, lime)
- **Feel:** Clean, European, sophisticated

### Beat Tape (general)
- **Option 1:** Monochromatic — all one hue, varying lightness
- **Option 2:** Duotone — two contrasting colors (black + neon)
- **Option 3:** Gradient — works well for house if subtle

## Typography Choices for Producer Branding

### Font Pairings for House Producers

| Style | Display Font | Body/Subtitle Font | Where to Get |
|-------|------------|------------------|-------------|
| Minimal/Modern | Helvetica Neue Bold | Helvetica Light | System font |
| Futuristic | Orbitron | Exo 2 | Google Fonts (free) |
| Vintage/Disco | Bebas Neue | Montserrat | Google Fonts (free) |
| Underground | Archivo Black | Archivo | Google Fonts (free) |
| Elegant | Playfair Display | Lato | Google Fonts (free) |

### Typography Rules
- **Producer name:** Largest or most prominent — your brand above the beat title
- **Beat title:** Second largest
- **BPM/Key info:** Smallest, can be omitted for clean designs
- **Never use more than 2 font families** in one piece
- **Avoid:** Comic Sans, Papyrus, Curlz, Impact (unless intentionally ironic)

## Free Tools

### Canva — [canva.com](https://www.canva.com)
- Best for fast, professional-looking cover art with no design experience
- Huge template library — search "album cover" or "music cover"
- Free tier: sufficient for most use cases
- Pro ($12.99/month): background remover, brand kit, premium templates

### Adobe Express — [express.adobe.com](https://express.adobe.com)
- Free tier, Adobe quality
- Good template selection for music content
- Integrates with Adobe Firefly AI (see AI section below)

### GIMP — [gimp.org](https://www.gimp.org)
- Free, open-source, professional-grade
- Steeper learning curve than Canva but full control
- Use for: advanced layering, texture work, photo manipulation

### Photoshop (Paid — industry standard)
- Used by professional designers
- Worth it if you're serious about branding quality
- Adobe Creative Cloud: $54.99/month (full suite) or $20.99/month (Photography plan)

### After Getting the AI Image
1. Download the best result (U1/U2/U3/U4 to upscale in Midjourney)
2. Bring into Canva or Photoshop
3. Add your producer name and beat/tape title using your brand fonts
4. Add your logo (lower corner, subtle)

### Adobe Firefly — [firefly.adobe.com](https://firefly.adobe.com)
- Free tier available (with watermark)
- Integrated with Adobe Express and Photoshop
- **Generative Fill:** Replace backgrounds, extend images — very useful for resizing AI images to exact spec

### DALL-E 3 via ChatGPT — [chatgpt.com](https://chatgpt.com)
- Accessible via ChatGPT Plus ($20/month)
- Good for quick concept art; less stylistically consistent than Midjourney
- Use for: rapid prototyping ideas before refining in Midjourney

## Brand Consistency Across Artwork

### Establish a Brand Style Guide (5 elements)
1. **Primary color:** 1 main brand color (e.g., electric blue #00A6FB)
2. **Secondary color:** 1 accent (e.g., off-black #0D0D0D)
3. **Font 1:** Display/headline font
4. **Font 2:** Body/subtitle font
5. **Logo:** BSP logo or your collective mark

Apply these consistently across ALL artwork.

### Logo Placement
- **Beat tape covers:** Centered or upper third, prominent
- **Single beat covers:** Small, bottom corner (beat title is the focus)
- **Thumbnails:** Upper right or lower left — clear on both light and dark backgrounds

## Cover Art by Product Type

### Beat Tape Cover
- Full artistic cover — tell a visual story
- Producer name prominent, tape name large, volume number
- High production value — this represents the whole collection
- Size: 3000x3000px JPEG for streaming + 1400x1400px for BeatStars

### Single Beat Cover (BeatStars)
- Can be simpler — a colored background + beat name + your logo
- Or use the AI art method above for a unique visual per beat
- Consistent color palette across all beats in a genre series

### Beat Bundle Cover
- Combine 3–4 single beat thumbnails into a grid layout
- "5 BEATS | $X" text overlay — functional and clear
- Match the genre aesthetic
