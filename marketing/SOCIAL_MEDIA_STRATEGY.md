# Social Media Strategy for Beat Sellers

---

## Instagram

### Beat Clip Format
- **Aspect ratio:** 9:16 (vertical) for Reels; 1:1 (square) for Feed posts
- **Length:** Reels: 30–90 seconds; Feed video: 60 seconds
- **Content:** The most energetic section of the beat (the drop) — hook the viewer in the first 3 seconds
- **Overlay text:** Beat name, BPM, genre, "Link in bio"
- **Audio quality:** Export at 320 kbps MP3; Instagram compresses audio so start high

### Posting Schedule
- **Feed posts:** 4–5x per week
- **Reels:** 1x per day (Reels get significantly more reach than feed posts)
- **Stories:** 5–10 per day (behind-the-scenes, polls, countdowns to beat drops)

### Story Templates for Beat Drops
Use Instagram's countdown sticker for beat drops:
1. Post a "New beat dropping in 24 hours" story with countdown timer
2. Post a 15-second snippet 2 hours before
3. Post the full Reel when it drops with a CTA sticker ("Swipe Up" or link sticker)
4. Post a poll: "Which beat should I drop next: A or B?"

### Reels Strategy
- **First 1–3 seconds** must be visually and sonically arresting — no slow intros
- Add trending audio as a background track in addition to your beat (Instagram allows layering)
- Use the "Green Screen" effect with your visualizer as background
- Post consistently for 30+ days before expecting algorithmic push

### Hashtag Strategy

Use 20–25 hashtags per post. Mix sizes:

| Type | Examples | Size |
|------|---------|------|
| Large (5M+ posts) | #housemusic #producer #beats | Very competitive |
| Medium (500K–5M) | #deephouse #beatmaker #houseproducer | Moderate |
| Small (10K–500K) | #techhouse2024 #housebeatsforsale #beatstars | More targetable |
| Niche | #housebeats #afrohouse #sellbeats | Small but targeted |

**Example set:**
```
#housemusic #deephouse #techhouse #beatmaker #producer
#housebeats #beatsforsale #beatmaker2024 #houseproducer
#beatstars #sellbeats #musicproducer #beatdrop
#flstudio #abletonlive #electronicmusic #dancemusic
#afrohouse #clubmusic #housemusician #beatmaking
#sounddesign #musicproduction #samplepack
```

### Collaborating with Artists
- Search Instagram for artists in your genre (house singers, rappers over beats)
- DM format: "Hey [Name], love your sound — made a beat that would suit your style. Would you want to hear it?"
- Offer a **free beat in exchange for a tag** in their video — valuable exposure
- Go for artists with 5K–100K followers (larger artists are harder to reach; smaller ones don't have the reach)

## TikTok

### Behind-the-Scenes Content (Best Performing)
- "Making a house beat in 60 seconds" — show your DAW, compressed into a Reel
- "Studio session at 2am" — late-night vibe resonates
- "Rating beats from my archive" — react to your old work
- "Producing a beat from scratch to finished mix" — 3-part series

### "Rate My Beat" Trend
1. Upload a 15-second clip of your beat
2. Ask "Rate this beat out of 10 in the comments"
3. Pin the best comment to increase engagement
4. Reply to every comment for the first hour — TikTok algorithm rewards quick engagement

### "Beats for Sale" Audio Hooks
- Start video with your beat playing immediately (no intro)
- First text overlay within 2 seconds: "This beat is for sale"
- Second overlay: genre + BPM
- End card: "Link in bio to purchase"

### Duet Strategy
1. Find artists who post acapella or freestyle videos (search #acapella, #freestyle, #singer)
2. Duet their video with your beat underneath
3. Tag them in the comments: "Made a beat for this — free if you want it"
4. This creates collaboration content and puts your music in front of their audience

### TikTok Posting Schedule
- **2–3x per day** for maximum growth (TikTok rewards frequency)
- Best times: 7–9am, 12–3pm, 7–11pm (your audience's timezone)

## YouTube

### Beat Tape Uploads
- Compile 10–15 beats into a **beat tape** (full YouTube video, 30–60 minutes)
- Title: "[Genre] Beat Tape [Year] - Free Download | BSP"
- Description: timestamps for each beat, BeatStars link, all social handles
- Thumbnail: professional beat tape cover art

### Individual Beat Uploads
- One video per beat (for SEO — each beat can rank individually)
- Title format: `[Mood] [Subgenre] Beat - "[Name]" | [BPM] BPM (Free/For Sale)`
- Tags: genre, BPM, mood, DAW used, "type beat" for secondary keywords

### Tutorial Content (Builds Credibility + Search Traffic)
- "How to make a deep house beat in FL Studio" — very searchable
- "Best free samples for house music 2024"
- "How to set up sidechain compression for house music"
- Tutorials rank in YouTube search and establish you as an authority

### YouTube Shorts
- Repurpose your TikTok content directly (9:16, 60 seconds max)
- Shorts appear in a separate feed with strong organic reach
- Title: use searchable terms ("how to make a house beat", "best house beat samples")

### Channel Structure
Create playlists:
- "Deep House Beats for Sale"
- "Tech House Beats"
- "Free Beats"
- "Production Tutorials"
- "Beat Tapes"

## Twitter / X

### Engaging with Artists and Producers
- Follow 20 new artists/producers per day in your target genre
- Retweet/quote tweet their music with genuine comments
- Reply to posts from artists who say "need beats" or "looking for producers"

### Beat Drop Posts
Format: tweet the beat clip or SoundCloud link + clear CTA
```
New deep house beat just dropped

BPM: 126 | Key: Am | Vibe: Late night

🎧 Link in bio (BeatStars)

#housemusic #deephouse #beatsforsale #producer
```

### Networking Tips
- **Twitter Spaces:** Join or host spaces in the music production community
- **#MusicMonday / #NewMusicFriday** — use these hashtags on release days
- Engage with major producers and music journalists — visibility compounds over time
- Don't spam links — ratio should be 80% engagement, 20% promotion

| Sat | Engagement: comment on 20 posts | Trending audio reel | YouTube Shorts | — | Graphics/covers |
| Sun | Story polls: vote next beat | — | — | Week recap | Planning next week |

### Content Batching (Recommended)
Rather than creating content daily, batch it weekly:
- **Tuesday:** Film all video content for the week (screen recordings, studio shots)
- **Wednesday:** Edit all clips, create thumbnails and graphics
- **Thursday:** Schedule all posts using Buffer or Later
- **Friday–Sunday:** Only engage (comments, replies, DMs)
