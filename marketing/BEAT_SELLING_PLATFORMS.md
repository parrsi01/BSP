# Beat Selling Platforms — Complete Guide

---

## BeatStars — [beatstars.com](https://www.beatstars.com)

The largest dedicated beat selling platform. Essential for any serious beat seller.

### Account Setup and Profile Optimization
1. Sign up at beatstars.com → choose **Pro Page** plan ($19.99/month) for your own URL and unlimited uploads
2. Profile photo: professional, consistent with your brand (1000x1000px minimum)
3. Banner: 2560x1440px — show your brand name, DAW, and genre
4. Bio: include your genre, DAW, notable clients, and a CTA ("DM for custom beats")
5. Link your social media, YouTube, and website

### Pricing Tiers (BeatStars Standard)

| License Type | Suggested Price | What It Includes |
|-------------|----------------|-----------------|
| Basic/Non-exclusive MP3 | $19.99–$39.99 | MP3, limited streams/distrib |
| Premium/Non-exclusive WAV | $49.99–$79.99 | WAV + MP3, more streams |
| Unlimited/Non-exclusive | $99.99–$149.99 | WAV + MP3, unlimited streams |
| Exclusive | $199.99–$499.99+ | Full rights transfer, WAV + Stems |
| Custom Exclusive | Negotiable | Negotiate directly |

**Tip:** Price slightly above competitors when starting. Low prices signal low quality. Start at $29.99 for MP3 non-exclusive and adjust based on data.

### Beat Tags (Watermarks)
- Your beat tag plays at key moments in the MP3 preview to prevent unauthorized use
- Place tag at: **0:30**, **1:00**, **1:30** in a 2-minute preview
- Tag audio: a short audio ident ("BSP" or your producer name) — 1–2 seconds
- Keep tags subtle enough not to be annoying but present enough to identify your work

### SEO: Keywords, Genre, and Mood Tags
BeatStars has internal search — optimization matters:

| Field | Example Inputs |
|-------|---------------|
| Genre | House, Deep House, Tech House, Afro House |
| Mood | Energetic, Dark, Groovy, Melodic, Underground |
| Keywords | 4-on-the-floor, sidechain, Chicago house, UK house |
| BPM | Enter exact BPM |
| Key | Enter key (e.g., A Minor) |

**SEO rule:** Use all available tag slots. Include the subgenre name in your beat title (e.g., "Deep House Beat - Warm Nights").

### Upload Checklist
- [ ] Beat title follows naming convention: `Genre - Beat Name` (e.g., "Deep House - Warm Nights")
- [ ] BPM and key filled in
- [ ] All genre/mood tags filled
- [ ] Beat tag placed in preview file (MP3)
- [ ] Stems uploaded for exclusive tier (ZIP file)
- [ ] Cover art uploaded (1000x1000px JPEG)
- [ ] All three license tiers priced
- [ ] Description written (60–150 words, include genre, mood, BPM, key)

## Airbit — [airbit.com](https://airbit.com)

### How Airbit Differs from BeatStars

| Feature | BeatStars | Airbit |
|---------|----------|--------|
| Market size | Larger | Smaller but growing |
| Pricing | Monthly fee | Free tier available |
| Interface | More feature-rich | Cleaner, simpler |
| Artist discovery | Better | Less mature |
| Analytics | Strong | Good |
| Direct checkout widget | Yes | Yes (embeddable) |

### Best Use Case for Airbit
- Use as a **secondary platform** alongside BeatStars
- Airbit's embeddable player/store widget is excellent for your own website
- The free tier allows up to 10 beats — good for testing the platform

### Pricing Strategy on Airbit
- Mirror your BeatStars prices exactly — consistency across platforms builds trust
- Occasional Airbit-exclusive discount codes to drive traffic

## Beatport / Traxsource / Juno Download

These are for **releasing finished tracks**, not selling beats to artists. They're relevant if you're producing complete house tracks.

### Beatport — [beatport.com](https://www.beatport.com)
- The #1 DJ download store for electronic music
- House music is core genre — strong audience
- **How to get listed:** You need a label (your own label or sign to existing). Use a distribution service.

### Traxsource — [traxsource.com](https://www.traxsource.com)
- House music-focused. More curated than Beatport.
- Preferred by deep house and soulful house artists
- **How to get listed:** Via a distributor with Traxsource relationships (DistroKid, TuneCore, Amuse)

### Juno Download — [junodownload.com](https://www.junodownload.com)
- UK-based, strong in house/techno/drum & bass
- Independent label-friendly
- **Distribution:** Direct label upload possible; or via distributor

### Distribution Services for Beatport/Traxsource

| Service | Cost | Beatport? | Traxsource? | Notes |
|---------|------|----------|------------|-------|
| DistroKid | $22.99/year | Yes | Yes | Fast, unlimited releases |
| TuneCore | $29.99/year | Yes | Yes | Per-release or annual |
| CD Baby | 9% commission | Yes | Yes | No annual fee option |
| Amuse | Free tier | Limited | Limited | Basic free tier |

## YouTube Monetization for Beats

### Upload Strategy
1. Upload beats as **YouTube videos** with visualizers (see graphics/VISUALIZER_GUIDE.md)
2. Title format: `[Mood] [Genre] Beat - "[Beat Name]" | [BPM] BPM`
   - Example: "Dark Tech House Beat - "Midnight Drive" | 128 BPM"
3. Description: include BeatStars/Airbit link, license info, all tags

### Monetization Methods

| Method | How | Revenue Potential |
|--------|-----|-----------------|
| AdSense ads | Enable monetization on channel | Low but passive |
| Beat sale links | BeatStars link in description | Direct revenue |
| YouTube Content ID | Block/monetize unauthorized uses of your beats | Moderate |
| Channel memberships | Exclusive beat drops for members | Moderate |

### Beat Video Format Tips
- Visualizer: 1920x1080, looping, clean
- Length: **3–5 minutes** minimum (YouTube favors longer videos for ads)
- Thumbnail: beat title + cover art + "FREE DOWNLOAD" or "BUY NOW" text
- End screen: link to BeatStars and another recent beat video

## SoundCloud — [soundcloud.com](https://soundcloud.com)

### Free Beat Drop Strategy
1. Upload a free beat (full quality, no tag) as a free download
2. Require a **follow + like** to unlock the download (SoundCloud gated download)
3. Builds followers, creates goodwill, and puts your beats in artists' hands
4. Include BeatStars link for the paid/exclusive version

### SoundCloud Go+ Monetization
- Available to creators in eligible countries
- Monetize streams when subscribers play your tracks
- Requires SoundCloud for Artists enrollment

### Repost Chains and Promotion
- Join **repost-for-repost** networks with other producers
- Use **SoundCloud Premier** if eligible — monetizes streams
- Comment on popular house music tracks (genuine engagement builds visibility)

## TikTok and Instagram Reels

### Short Clip Strategy (15–60 seconds)
1. Cut a **30–60 second clip** of the best section of your beat (the drop)
2. Add minimal text: beat name, BPM, "LINK IN BIO"
3. Use trending audio sparingly — original audio builds your brand sound
4. Post when your audience is active (check analytics: typically evenings and weekends)

### TikTok Sound Licensing
- When you upload original audio to TikTok, others can use it in their videos
- This generates awareness but does **not** generate royalties by default
- To monetize original sounds: apply for **TikTok Creator Marketplace** when eligible
- **SoundOn** (TikTok's distribution arm) allows artists to upload music and collect TikTok royalties

## Direct Sales: Your Own Website

### Platform Options

| Platform | Cost | Best For |
|----------|------|---------|
| [Gumroad](https://gumroad.com) | 10% fee (free tier) | Simple digital downloads |
| [Payhip](https://payhip.com) | 5% fee (free tier) | Beat bundles, easy setup |
| Shopify | $29/month | Professional storefront |
| Squarespace | $16/month | Combined portfolio + store |
| WordPress + WooCommerce | Hosting cost | Full customization |

### Why Your Own Website Matters
- 0% platform fees (vs BeatStars' cut)
- Full control over your customer data and email list
- Builds brand authority
- Cross-sell products (sample packs, presets, tutorials)

### Email List Building from Your Store
- Offer a **free beat for email signup** via Mailchimp or ConvertKit popup
- Every purchase should auto-add the buyer to your email list
- Email list = direct line to customers without algorithm interference
